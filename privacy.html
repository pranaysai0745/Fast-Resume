Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how FastResume collects, uses and protects your information when you use the website and resume builder.

Last updated: December 2025

Information we collect

FastResume may collect the following types of information:

  • Basic usage data such as pages visited and time spent (via Google Analytics).
  • Resume content that you type or paste into the builder.

How we use information

  • To generate resumes requested by users.
  • To improve website performance and user experience.
  • To analyse traffic and usage trends in an aggregated way.

Resume content

Resume text that you enter is processed only for generating the resume preview and output. It is not intended to be permanently stored in a database. However, temporary technical logs may exist for debugging and reliability.

Cookies & similar technologies

FastResume may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and advertising purposes through services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense.

Third-party services

  • Google Analytics – to understand how visitors use the website.
  • Google AdSense – to display advertising on the site.

Your choices

  • You can clear your browser cookies if you do not want information stored locally.
  • You may choose not to use the builder if you are not comfortable sharing resume content.
