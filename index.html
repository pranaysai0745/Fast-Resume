How the builder works

No complicated steps. Just bring your details – the builder and AI will handle the structure and wording.

Step 1

Share your details

Go to the builder, paste your education, skills, projects, internships and achievements as a simple paragraph or bullet list.

Step 2

Let AI organise everything

FastResume sends your text to Groq’s Llama 3 model and arranges it into sections like Summary, Skills, Projects and Education automatically.

Step 3

Preview, tweak & download

Edit any field manually, switch templates and export a neat PDF ready to upload to campus portals and job sites.
