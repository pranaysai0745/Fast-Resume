About FastResume

FastResume is an AI-powered resume builder created especially for students and freshers. It helps you go from raw text to a clean, ATS-friendly resume in a few clicks.

Who is FastResume for?

FastResume is designed especially for:

B.Tech / B.E. CSE students IT & software freshers Final-year project teams Students applying for internships Campus placement preparation

Why FastResume?

Simple to use

Paste one paragraph about yourself and let the AI split it into sections like summary, education, projects, and skills.

Student-focused

The system is tuned for students and entry-level roles: more importance to projects, internships and skills instead of long job history.

No signup required

You can directly open the builder, generate your resume and download it. No login, no account, no password.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FastResume free?

Yes, FastResume is completely free for students and freshers to use.

Do I need to create an account?

No login or signup is required. You can build a resume instantly.

Is my data stored anywhere?

Resume data is processed only for generating the resume. It is not permanently stored in a database.

Which AI model are you using?

FastResume uses the Groq API with Llama 3 to generate and clean resume content, tuned specifically for fresher profiles.
