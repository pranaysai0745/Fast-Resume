Need help with FastResume?

If you face an issue with the builder or have ideas to improve it, you can contact the creator directly. Clear feedback from students makes the tool better for everyone.

Common questions

Can you edit my resume personally?
FastResume is mainly a self-service tool. Personal resume editing is not guaranteed, but strong suggestions and feedback are always welcome.
I found a bug in the builder. What should I do?
Please send a short description plus a screenshot (if possible) of what happened. Mention your browser (Chrome, Edge, etc.) and what you were trying to do.
Can I suggest new features?
Yes! You can suggest new templates, extra sections (like achievements), better export options and more. Good student-focused ideas are always welcome.
