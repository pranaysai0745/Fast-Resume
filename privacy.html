    Privacy Policy

    How FastResume handles your data when you use the AI resume builder.

    Last updated: December 2025

    FastResume (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a simple, student-focused resume builder. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

    1. What data do we collect?

    Information you type into the site

    When you use the builder, you may type details such as your name, contact information, education, skills, projects, and experience. This text is used only to generate your resume content.

    Technical / usage data

    Like most websites, basic technical information may be collected automatically, such as:

    • Browser type and version
    • Approximate region (based on IP)
    • Pages visited and basic usage statistics

    This is mainly used in aggregated form to understand how students use the site and to keep the service reliable.

    2. How is your resume text used?

    Your resume text is sent to our AI provider (Groq API using the Llama 3 model) only for the purpose of generating or improving your resume. We do not sell your resume data.

    We do not intentionally store your resume content in a database. Your text is held temporarily in memory while the AI response is processed and rendered on the page.

    3. Cookies & third-party services

    Analytics

    We may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how many students use FastResume, which pages are popular, and to improve the product over time.

    Advertising

    FastResume may show ads through Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies to serve more relevant ads. You can manage or opt-out of personalized advertising through your Google account settings.

    4. Data retention

    We aim to keep only the minimum data required for operating the site, debugging problems, and basic analytics. Resume text is not intended to be stored long-term on our servers.

    5. Children’s privacy

    FastResume is mainly designed for college and university students. If you are under the age required by the laws in your country to use such services, please use FastResume only with guidance from a parent, guardian, or teacher.

    6. Your choices

    • You can choose not to enter real personal details while testing.
    • You can clear your text from the builder any time using the “Clear” option.
    • You can block or clear cookies in your browser settings.

    7. Contact

    If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, or want to report a data-related issue, please email:

    Email: pranaysaibly@gmail.com

    8. Changes to this policy

    This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally as the product grows or if we add new features (for example, login or saved resumes). When we change the policy, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
