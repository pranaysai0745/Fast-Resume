About FastResume

FastResume is a simple, free AI resume builder created to help students and freshers generate clean, ATS-friendly resumes in a few minutes.

Focused on students Built by an engineering student Powered by Groq · Llama 3
Goal Make resume building easy

Many students struggle with:

  • What to write in each section
  • How to make the resume look clean
  • Aligning content to ATS requirements

FastResume helps by taking one paragraph about you and turning it into a structured, professional resume you can edit and download.

What is FastResume?

FastResume is a web app where you can paste your details once and instantly get a formatted resume. It is tuned especially for:

  • B.Tech / B.E. CSE and IT students
  • Freshers applying for their first job
  • Students preparing for internships and campus placements

How does it work?

FastResume is built with a Node.js backend and the Groq API:

  • You paste one paragraph about your education, skills, projects and internships.
  • The backend sends this to Groq (Llama 3) with a carefully designed prompt.
  • The model returns a structured JSON resume (summary, education, skills, projects, etc.).
  • The frontend shows a live preview with multiple templates you can switch between.
  • You can edit any field and finally export using the browser’s “Print / Save as PDF”.

About the creator

FastResume is built and maintained by a student developer who wanted a simple way for classmates and juniors to build resumes without struggling with Word templates.

The goal is to keep the tool:

  • Simple – no complex signup or onboarding
  • Student-friendly – tuned for fresher profiles
  • Free – supported by minimal ads
