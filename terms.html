About

AI resume builder made for students.

FastResume is a tiny side-project built by a student, for students. It turns one paragraph of your story into a clean, ATS-friendly resume you can send to internships, campus placements and first-job openings.

Who is FastResume for?

FastResume is tuned especially for:

B.Tech / B.E. CSE & IT students Software & IT freshers Final-year project teams Students applying for internships Campus placement preparation
Simple to use Paste one paragraph about yourself, and let the AI split it into sections like profile summary, education, projects, experience and skills.
Student-focused The system gives more importance to projects, internships and skills instead of long job history — perfect for students and first-job seekers.
No signup required You can directly open the builder, generate your resume and download it. No login, no password, no payment.

How FastResume works

1. Paste your story once
Write in your own words — CGPA, skills, projects, internships, certifications, languages and interests.
2. AI structures the resume
FastResume uses the Groq API with Llama 3 to organize the content, improve wording slightly and keep it student-friendly.
3. You tweak & download
Edit any field manually, choose a layout template, then export to PDF using your browser’s print dialog.
