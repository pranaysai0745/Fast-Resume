How it works

Step 1
Paste your details
Go to the builder page and paste one paragraph about your skills, education, projects, internships, and certifications.
Step 2
Let AI clean and format
FastResume sends it to Groq (Llama 3), rewrites content in professional English, and fills each resume section automatically.
Step 3
Download & apply
Choose a template, tweak any field, then print or save to PDF and use it for campus placements, internships, or job portals.
