FastResume for students & freshers

FastResume is a small student-built tool to help other students create clean, ATS-friendly resumes without struggling with formatting or long registration forms.

The goal of FastResume is simple: you paste one paragraph about yourself, and the AI turns it into a structured resume with sections like Summary, Skills, Education, Projects and Internships.

Instead of focusing on long job history, FastResume pays more attention to projects, academic work, online courses and extracurricular activities – exactly what matters for students and first-job seekers.

🎓 Built for B.Tech & college students ⚙️ Powered by Groq · Llama 3 📄 Export as PDF 🧪 Student side-project
Who is FastResume for? Students in engineering, IT and related fields, freshers applying for internships, and first-job seekers preparing for campus placements.
What makes it different? A very small, focused tool – no account, no complicated screens. Just AI support, neat templates and a fast way to get a presentable resume.
What does the AI do? It rewrites rough notes into professional language, groups bullet points and keeps the layout readable for recruiters and ATS systems.

Is FastResume free?
Yes. The builder is free for students and freshers. There is no paid plan right now.
Do I need to create an account?
No login or signup is required. You can open the builder and generate a resume directly.
Is my data stored?
Resume content is processed only for generating the resume preview. It is not permanently stored in a database.
