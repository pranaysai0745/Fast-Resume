About FastResume

FastResume is an AI-powered resume builder designed especially for students, freshers, and early-career job seekers. Our goal is to simplify resume creation and help candidates generate ATS-friendly resumes within minutes.

Why FastResume?

  • No signup or login required
  • Designed specifically for students & freshers
  • Optimised for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
  • Free and privacy-friendly

What makes it different

AI-powered formatting Uses Groq + Llama 3 to clean, structure, and improve resume content automatically.
Student-focused prompts Emphasis on projects, skills, and internships—no fake experience.
Instant preview & download Edit, preview, and download resumes as PDF directly from the browser.

Our mission

FastResume is built and maintained as an educational and productivity platform. We believe every student deserves a professional resume without paying fees or sharing personal data.
